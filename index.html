Government Senior Allowance

The government provides social security benefits to the Dalit community aged 60 years and non-Dalit community aged 70 years and above.

Guidelines!

  • > The person applying for Senior Allowance must be 60 years old.
  • > The person applying for Senior Allowance must reside in Nepal.
  • > The person applying for Senior Allowance must have an income below Rs. 10,000 per month.

Application Process

  • > Copy of citizenship certificate.
  • > Copy of birth certificate.
  • > Copy of income certificate.

Eligibility Criteria

  • > Visual impairment
  • > Hearing impairment
  • > Physical disability

What is Senior Allowance?

Senior Allowance is a government scheme that provides a yearly allowance to citizens aged 60 and above on an annual basis.
